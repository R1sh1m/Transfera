# Privacy Policy

Transfera is a desktop application that runs entirely on your local machine. We do not collect, transmit, or upload any of your data, files, or metadata to any server.

## How the App Works

Transfera helps you copy or move photos and videos from connected devices (such as iPhones or local folders) to a destination folder you choose on your computer. The app accesses files on those devices only for the purpose of this transfer. Nothing is sent over the network.

## Local Storage

Thumbnails and a transfer history database are stored in the app's local data directory on your machine. This information never leaves your computer. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data.

## No Tracking

Transfera contains no analytics, no telemetry, and no crash reporting. There are no background services phoning home, no usage statistics collected, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

## Metadata Handling

Transfera uses ExifTool, a widely used open-source utility, to read metadata from your media files (such as capture dates and camera settings). All metadata processing happens locally on your machine. No metadata is transmitted anywhere.

## Questions

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue at [github.com/R1sh1m/Transfera/issues](https://github.com/R1sh1m/Transfera/issues).
